# UJaaka Umlobi

##
NguJaaka lo, umlobi
waseNeminya.

##
Uloba kumlambo
iNayile.

##
Unesikhephe, umnatha
negwegwe.

##
Uvuka ekuseni rhoqo
ukuze abambe intlanzi
yaseNayile.

##
Ngentsimbi yeshumi,
uthengisa iintlanzi
emarikeni eJunja.

##
Kwenzeke ntoni, Jakaa?
Ibalekile intlanzi?
Unxunguphele.

##
"Izinto azihambi
kakuhle namhlanje.
Umoya waseNayile
awundivumeli ndilobe.
Enye intlanzi imke
negwegwe lam."

##
"Kodwa ndinethemba
lokuba kuzobangcono
ngomso, ngoba
ndithenge igwegwe
elitsha."
