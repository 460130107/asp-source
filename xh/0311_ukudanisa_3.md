# Ukudanisa Mary Nabire and Tessa Welch

##
Ndidanisa xa
ndonwabile,
Ndidanisa xa
ndibuhlungu,
Ndidanisa xa
ndindedwa,
Ndidanisa xa
ndicaphukile.

##
Utitshalakazi wam uthi,
“Hlala phantsi uzole!
Sukudanisa imini
yonke.”

##
Kodwa umama wam
yena uthi,
“Ukudanisa kwenza ube
sempilweni,
Ukudanisa kwenza ube
mde,
Ngenye imini uza kuba
ngumdanisi,
Kwaye uza
kubamangalisa bonke!”

##
Kwaye mna ndithi,
“Ngenye imini ndiza
kudanisa kwaye abantu
baza kubhatala
xa befuna ukundibona
nditsiba-tsiba
kwaye ndizibhija-bhija”
...
‘Udanisa kakuhle
KAKHULU’, baza
kutsho.”
