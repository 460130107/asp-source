# Amaphulo enkomo yodumo

##
Le yinkomo.
Inkomo intle!

##
Emini yinkomo
eqhelekileyo,

##
Ibhabhisa iKhayihi,

##
Okanye ikhaba ibhola.

##
Ebusuku inkomo
ebalaseleyo!

##
Isindisa imiphefumlo,

##
Kwaye ilwa ulwaphulomthetho.

##
Kodwa, ukuba uvala amehlo akho ngokuqinileyo ngenene,
kwaye wenze Umnqwno -

##
Inkomo ingeza Kukubona nawe ngokunjala!

##
Isiphetho.
