# Sherry Muhutale

##
Sane at home.
Sherry ali engo.


##
It is Sane’s birthday.
Oludaalo luno njo
lubbasaliyeho Sherry.


##
Sane at the bus stop.
Sherry ali ebbasi aŋajemelera


##
Sane in the bus.
Sherry ali mu bbasi


##
Sane at the market.
Sherry muhatale.


##
Ooh! Many fruits.
ooh! ebibala bingi.


##
Sweet apples.
emiyembe ejiŋoma.


##
But where is mother
mother?
Naye maama aliŋena?


##
I went to buy a birthday
present for you.
Njiye ohuhugulira
ehirabo hya mazalibwa.


##
Thank you mum!

webale maama!
