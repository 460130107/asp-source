# A fly

##
A fly is an insect.

##
A fly lives in dirty
places

##
In toilets,rubbish pits
and on food.

##
It flies from dirty places
to our homes.

##
It falls on our food and
makes it dirty.

##
when one eats dirty food, he gets
stomach ache.

##
A fly is very dangerous.
