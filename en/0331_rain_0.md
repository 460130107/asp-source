# Rain

##
I love water.

##
I enjoy playing with
water.

##
It is raining.

##
Rain gives us water.

##
I enjoy playing outside.

##
But it is raining.

##
I enjoy playing out in
the rain.

##
My hair is wet from
playing in the rain.
My father is fixing my
hair.
