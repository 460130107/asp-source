##
One day, Rabbit was
walking by the
riverside.

##
Hippo was there too,
going for a stroll and
eating some nice green
grass.

##
Hippo didn't see that
Rabbit was there and
she accidentally
stepped on Rabbit's
foot.
Rabbit yelled and
started screaming at
Hippo, “You Hippo!
Can't you see that
you're stepping on my
foot?”

##
Hippo apologised to
Rabbit, “I'm so sorry,
my friend. I didn't see
you. Please forgive
me!”
But Rabbit wouldn't
listen and he shouted at
Hippo, “You did that on
purpose! Someday,
you'll see! You're going
to pay!”
