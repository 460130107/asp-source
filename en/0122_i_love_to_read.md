# I love to read

##
I love to read

##
Who can I read for?

##
The baby is sleeping

##
Who can I read for?

##
Mom and grandmother
are cooking

##
Who can I read for?

##
Father and grandfather
are fixing the car

##
Who can I read for?
I will sit and read for
myself
