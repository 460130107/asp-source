# Accident

##
I saw a big truck driving
down the road.

##
A car came driving up
the road.

##
The truck driver was
talking on his
cellphone.

##
He was not looking at
the road.

##
There was a big
accident.

##
An ambulance and the
police came to help.

##
Two tow trucks came.
They towed the truck
and the car.

##
I was shocked.
