# A Trip to Zanzibar

##
A walk in Zanzibar,
what do you see?
Shops selling sweets,
in very narrow streets.

##
A swim in Zanzibar,
what do you see?
Changu, Kibua, Tasi,
Pono, Jodari na Kambisi.

##
A drive in Zanzibar,
what do you see?
Animals in a jungle,
watching at an angle

##
A dance in Zanzibar,
what do you see?
Swahili, English and Arab,
all dancing Taarab.

##
A drink in Zanzibar,
what do you see?
miwa, madafu juicy,
coffee and tea are spicy.

##
A trip to Zanzibar,
do you wish to go back?
