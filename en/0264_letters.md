# Letters

##
Last week I received a
lot of letters.

##
On Monday my doctor
wrote a letter to my
teacher.

##
Thabisile is sick. She
must stay at home.

##
On Tuesday I got a
letter from my class.

##
Get well soon Thabisile!

##
On Wednesday I got a
letter from my best
friend.

##
I drew this picture for
you. Love from Dudu.

##
On Thursday I got a
letter from my mother.
