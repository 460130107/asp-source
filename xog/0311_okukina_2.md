# Okukina
Mary Nabire and Tessa
Welch

##
Nkina bwemba
isanhu,
Nkina bwemba
naku,
Nkina bwemba
gholongera,
Nkina bwemba


ndi
ndi
ndi
ndi ilalu.

##
Omusomesa wange
ankoba,
"Tyama oteleere!
Tokinhiriza buli kiseera."


##
Aye Maama ankoba,
"Okukina kukulamya,
Okukina kukuwanvuya,
Lulala oidha kuba
mukini,
Obeewunise
bonaboona!"


##
Nze naakoba,
"Lulala ndha kukina
abantu basasulire
Okubona nze nga ndi
kubuuka bwe neetala.
‘Akina lubabu,’ bwe
baidha okukoba."
