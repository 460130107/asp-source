# Amayumba

##
Wakabi aba mu nhumba
nsereke ya isubi.

##
Kirunda aba mu
nhumba ya nsoda.

##
Enhumba enho ya
muserebende.

##
Akasolya ke nhumba
eno kansoda isatu.

##
Omusadha onho ali
kwezimbira ye mwene
enhumba.

##
Enhumba eno mpyaka.

##
Enhumba eno nkaire.

##
Nandyenze mbe
owomunhumba eno.
