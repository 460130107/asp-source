# Akhapao khanje khali yena?

##
Engosiye akhapao
khanje.
Ne ekhairwe
okhukhanyola.

##
Ekhabire inyuma wo
muliango.
Shikhabereyo tawe.

##
Ekhabire
khumusereswa .
Shekhanyolirekho ta.

##
Ekhabire mulwalo
weshitali. Kata hosi,
khabulao.

##
Ekhabire mukari
welisanduku.
Eshiechesiakho mbu
khalimo shibulileho.

##
Ekhabire mushisuli
mwosi.

##
Ekhabire ano, alia
nende buli abundu,
shenyolirekho abundu
wosi tawe.
