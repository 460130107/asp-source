# Akhapao khanje
khali yena?
Meera Tendolkar and
Madhav Chavan

##
Engosiye akhapao
khanje.
Ne ekhairwe
okhukhanyola.


##
Ekhabire inyuma wo
muliango.
Shikhabereyo tawe.


##
Ekhabire
khumusereswa .
Shekhanyolirekho ta.


##
Ekhabire mulwalo
weshitali. Kata hosi,
khabulao.


##
Ekhabire mukari
welisanduku.
Eshiechesiakho mbu
khalimo shibulileho.


##
Ekhabire mushisuli
mwosi.


##
Ekhabire ano, alia
nende buli abundu,
shenyolirekho abundu
wosi tawe.
