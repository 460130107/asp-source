# Co Katinyiŋ

##
Limtocini
mukulotyonyimwani
tokuuk ceecaaŋ.

##
Aŋaraci ani yee
kamwowee koko
ceeptyaŋkocok
kaseleny.

##
Akasekey nyee
kaaparpariti yee
kayoren akoo corweet
nyimwani.

##
Akase kwapi yee
kamwoy papa nyimwani
kule maponte karik.

##
Akase Kule /ceecaamoo
yee kaaŋatuta yeyo
nyimwani.
