His sister is looking at
the clothes in shops.
Sipho is bored.

##
Kakay Sipho kito ake
nyee caamet.

##
Colyoi Kwoyok.

##
Kasyeem neeto kwoyok
tokol.

##
Kakwoolyi ceekwaam
kwooyookap nopiret.

##
Amune sakusartakey
cektoka wunyi?

##
Kupeyok kule, kuponta
neeto wule lworone
mpiret lakwart.
Miite neeto kulole
kwoyokyi coo leelac.
