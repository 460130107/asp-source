# Melɔ̃a nuxexlẽ

##
Melɔ̃a nuxexlẽ.

##
Ame kae mate ŋu axlẽ nu na?

##
Nɔvinye nyɔnu dɔ alɔ̃.

##
Ame kae mate ŋu axlẽ nu na?

##
Danye kple mamanye womekpɔ vovo o.

##
Ame kae mate ŋu axlẽ nu na?

##
Fofonye kple tɔgbuinye womekpɔ vovo o.

##
Ame kae mate ŋu axlẽ nu na?

Mate ŋu axlẽ nu na ɖokuinye!
