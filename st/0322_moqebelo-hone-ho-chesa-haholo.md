# Moqebelo hone ho chesa haholo

##
Motseare oa mantsibua ka
khoeli ea ts'itioe hone ho chesa
haholoholo.
Emong le emong o ne a sa
thaba.
‘Bontle mpho le Lerato ba
khotha hotsoala kantle ho ea
bapala,' ‘M'e oa bona are
‘hobona, hakebatle haleka
bapalla tlasa maoto aka,
batsoela kantle hontlo.'

##
‘Hare eng nokeng,' Lerato a re
ho pholile moo. ‘Empa Mme a re
reseke satole ka nokeng,' ho
rialo Botle.
‘Harena hotola,' ho araba
Lerato, ‘re tlo bapala mono
moreting pela metsi.'

##
Empa re bapala libeke. O
chesang mosebetsi leha hole
joalo.
Pele noka moo retla rola lieta
tsa rona pele.
Empa hontse hochesa.
Eaba re hlobola lihempe le
mese ea rona,
Empa hontse hole joalo hoba
hontse ho chesa.

##
Eaba bakenya maotoa a bona
ka nokeng hore a phoole.
Ba hasana ka metsi.
Ka mora nako e seng kae
baiphumana base bale metsi
hohle.

##
‘oh tloho retlo tola,' ho rialo
Mpho, ‘ha ana hotseba Mme.'
Batola, baba batola ba
qetelletse ba lebetse nako.

##
Letsatsi laqala hoea malikelong,
be qala hohatsela.
Liphahlo tsa rona li kae?

##
Re li shebile tlasa lifate.
Ra li sheba le limeleng tse
ngata, hohle re ile ra sheba.

##
Pela noka moo, honeng hoena le likhomo lintse
li itjella joang bo bonolo.
Bontle a sheba holimo, ‘shebang khomo eane!
Entse ejang?'
‘Entse eja palesa e khubelu,' horialo Lerato.

##
A se palesa e khubelu,' ho rialo Mpho ka
bohale, ke hempe ea hao!'
Rasheba khomo engoe hape, ba fumana entse
eja ntho engoe e putsoa.
‘Ke mose oaka,' horialo Botle ka bohale.

##
Baile baea ea hae , ba te'ne
mekoarela fela, bahatsetse hoo
ebileng ba thothomelang. Empa
ene ese hobane ho pholile.
‘Ke hobane likhomo tseno lijele
liphahlo tsa bona,' joale balla.
Oile oa re kholoa Ntate?
Kamora nako eseng kae eaba
litono tsa rona lia oma, empa
line lisa ome hobane li omisoa
ke letsatsi!
