# Chibarwa

##
Lichonekha nanyolile
chibarwa chingali.

##
Olwabarasa omusilihi
wase kaandikile
omwalimu wase
ebarwa.

##
Wamakina alikho
aluala. Harama engo.

##
Mu mulimo kibili
nanyolile ebarwa
khukhwama mukilasi
yase.

##
Sosa bwangu
Wamakina!

##
Lwakhataru nanyolile
ebarwa khukhwama wo
musale wange wo
kumwoyo.

##
Nakhuchorera ewe
sifwanani sino. Busime
khukhwama khu
Wandanje.

##
Lwokhune nanyolile
ebwarwa khukhwama
khu mayi wase.
