# Basooleli babili
be ntsala
Clare Verbeek, Thembani
Dladla and Zanele
Buthelezi

##
Wantsala ni Wanda bali
basooleli babili be
ntsala.


##
Kuukhu ali khuukhola
tsikeeki likhumi
watsireere khu sowaani.


##
Wantsala waabukulakho
ikeeki indwela
ne Wanda
waabukulakho tsibili.


##
Wantsala waabukula
tsitsiindi tsine.
Wanda naye waabukula
tsibili.


##
Wantsala alya tsikeeki
tsenga?
?
Ne Wanda alya tsenga?
?
Balekhela kuukhu
tsenga?
