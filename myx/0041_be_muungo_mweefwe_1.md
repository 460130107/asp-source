# Be muungo
mweefwe
Clare Verbeek, Thembani
Dladla and Zanele
Buthelezi

##
Waandayase Kutosi ali
khuusoma shitabo sye
lileekelo.


##
Maayi ali khuusoma i
Bayibuli.


##
Umukooko wase Nelima
ali khuusoma shitabo
shishikanikha khu bye
khuuteekha.


##
Paapa waandaye wa
paapa ali khuusoma
luupapula lwe
kamakhuuwa.


##
Kuukhu ali khuusoma
shiitabo.


##
Umukooko wase
umukyeekhe Nekesa
naye ali khuusoma
shiitabo she
bifwaananyi.


##
Indi khuusoma shiitabo
shino.


##
Imbuka inoola isome
buli shiindu.
