# Tsibarwa
Clare Verbeek, Thembani
Dladla and Zanele
Buthelezi

##
Isapiiti iyabirire nafunile
tsibarwa tsingali.


##
Khu palatsa umukaangi
wase aaronela
umuleekeli wase
ibarwa.


##
Nafuuna mulwaale.
Aakha arame ingo.


##
Khu lwa khabiri nafuna
ibarwa khukhwaama isi
baasomi bashaase.


##
Uwumbuutse khaangu,
Nafuuna.


##
Lwa khataru nafuna
ibarwa khukhwaama isi
musaale wase naabi.


##
Nakhuupile iwe shifaani
shino.
Ni khuukana khuukali
khukhwaama isi Dada.


##
Lwa khane nafuna
ibarwa khukhwaama isi
maayi.
