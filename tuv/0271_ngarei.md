# Ng'arei

##
Edisak ng'akan ng'aarei neting'io.

##
Edisak ng'akejen ng'aarei napetoro.

##
Ng'akonyen nachik ng'aarei ing'olikinete.

##
Ng'aki ng'aarei nachik epupete.

##
And two loving arms to HUG!

##
Ta anuakin ang'akan ang'arei alakara.
