# Ebikoroto Ebiyia

##
Sipho goes to town with
his sister.

##
Mosubati omwabo
akaba gakorigereria
chianga ase chituka.
Osipho akarosa sana.

##

gete ekemo keria
anchete.

##

biarenge kooniwa.
